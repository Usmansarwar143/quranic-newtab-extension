About the Developer

This beautiful Chrome New Tab Extension was developed by Usman Sarwar, a passionate developer who loves creating useful and aesthetically pleasing tools.

The extension features a clean, minimalist design with glassmorphism effects, real-time weather data, dynamic Quranic verses, and customizable quick links.

All data is stored locally in your browser for privacy, and the extension works offline with fallback features.
